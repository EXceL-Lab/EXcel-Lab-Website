About Our Lab

Efficient, Scalable and Accelerated Learning Laboratory (EXceL-Lab) is affiliated with the University of Central Florida (UCF), located in Orlando, FL. As part of the Department of Computer Science and the Institute of AI, our mission is to pioneer the next generation of efficient and scalable machine intelligence, with the ultimate goal of democratizing and accelerating AI technology.

Our EXceL-Lab actively collaborates with other laboratories and industry partners, and we are always seeking strong collaborators and motivated prospective Ph.D. students to join our team. Please contact Prof. Yuzhang Shang at yuzhang.shang@ucf.edu with your CV for open positions.
